About me

I'm a Game Designer and Data Specialist from Algeria, working in VALORANT Esports. I love helping VALORANT teams reach their potential and excel at the game.

I offer services that are tailored to elevate the performance and success of competitive VALORANT teams. Services include indentification of areas of improvement, tactical weaknesses and strategic opportuinities. I provide comprehnsive reports and breakdowns that highlight map control, economies' management and agent selections, as well as opponents' weakneses, strengths and how to gain an edge over them. The goal is to empower teams with knowledge and insights needed to reach their potential, adapt to the ever-evolving meta and achieve success at the highest levels.
